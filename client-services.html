Acquisitions & Shipping
All Minted garments are dispatched from our facilities in India. Standard domestic acquisitions take 3-5 business days. International transit times vary by destination. You will receive a tracking dossier once your parcel has been handed over to our couriers.
Garment Care
To preserve the structural integrity and heavy-weight drape of your Minted pieces, we recommend dry cleaning or cold-washing inside out. Lay flat to dry. Do not apply heat directly to any printed or embroidered crests.
Returns & Exchanges
We accept returns on unworn, unaltered items with all tags and original packaging intact within 14 days of delivery. Due to the limited nature of our drops, direct exchanges are subject to inventory availability. Please contact the Concierge to initiate a return.
Authenticity & Drops
Minted operates on a strictly limited release schedule. Once a collection is archived, it will not be restocked. To ensure authenticity, only acquire Minted garments directly through our official boutique or sanctioned partners.
